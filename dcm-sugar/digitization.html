Digitising the Sugar Value Chain and Services

Distributing Through Digital

With deep investments in digital platforms to enhance efficiency we are making the last-mile challenge in the sugar value chain easier. One of the first to adopt digital transfer of dues to growers, we have used WhatsApp extensively, created special apps, and made tracking digital and efficient. Co-creating with financial services to provide financial inclusion benefits to even the smallest landholders.
