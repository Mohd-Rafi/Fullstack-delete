Rural Development

Across four locations – Hariawan, Loni, Ajbapur and Rupapur we are implementing a unique Model village program that marries sectoral development programs with focused infrastructure development activities in select villages. This includes building infrastructure for Safe drinking water facilities, upgradation of Anganwadis, Schools, Primary Health Centers, Panchayat Bhawans etc. These activities are undertaken in close association & participation of the local community & Gram Panchayats.
