Sugar

Setting Benchmarks, Catalysing Change.

Situated in the heart of India’s sugarcane farming districts, partnering with over 2,25,000 growers, we produce about 700 million kilograms of sugar annually. Across 4 state-of-the-art, fully integrated sugar manufacturing units in Hardoi & Lakshimpur Kheri districts of Uttar Pradesh. Making us one of India’s leading sugar producers.

We have among the highest manufacturing efficiencies and safety, quality and environmental certifications. Our research and development and customer-centric approach has made us the preferred partner of marquee FMCG brands like Coca Cola, Pepsi, HUL, and Britannia.

What Makes Us Succeed

It is our extremely high levels of engagement with growers. From sowing to harvesting, we continuously interact to improve yields, ensure soil integrity and ensure quality.

We are also leading the change towards making sugar manufacturing sustainable and circular. With zero-wastage and 100% by-product utilization by creating new products that lower environmental impact.

Processes:

ISO 9001 / FSSC 22000 / SMETA / SGP / Halal Certified.

Safety and Environment:

ISO 9000, ISO 14000 and OHSAS 18000
