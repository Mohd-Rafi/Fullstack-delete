Employee Speak

“Learning That Technology is All About The People Who Use It”

As someone who belongs to the engineering team, I’ve also recognized the importance of designing user-friendly systems. . On the technology front, it’s impossible to design something useful without listening and understanding the end-user inputs of those who have to operate these systems day in and day out. You must talk to the operator, the technician, and the helper, for they bring their own experience. All interventions are aimed at enhancing operational efficiency and workplace safety..

Atul Kumar Srivastava Deputy GM – Engineering
Sugar Unit- Loni District Hardoi, U.P.

“It’s The Pats On The Back That Pushed Me Forward”

I started out as an accounts support staff and later became an accountant when my manager encouraged me to upscale my knowledge and take more responsibility.. In 2011, when my brother and mother passed away suddenly, I got a call from the MDs which reinforced the caring and supportive culture that is in the DNA of the organization.

Chitra Sharma Joint Manager- Finance & Accounts
Sugar Business, New Delhi

“I realized I am working for the planet”

Sustainability is a key index for any business to keep growing and for ensuring that every stakeholder’s interest is aligned. My experience with government institutions on sustainable sugarcane production has been very satisfying and rewarding . . Our partner institutions recognise and acknowledge our long-term values and commitment in all our farm and farmer-focused programs, especially on soil and water conservation. While the expectations from stakeholders and community was aspirational, , we could achieve the same with our meticulous planning and execution excellence.

Raja Srivastava Jt. Vice President,
Sugar Business, New Delhi

“Grow by making yourself redundant”

At DCM Shriram, we have a culture of investing in resources, developing their capability continuously.

As a female Graduate Engineering Trainees (GET) working at the plant, I was never experienced any bias and over the years, have grown professionally with enlarged role and new assignments. . There are many people who think that the factory environment in non-metro areas is not safe for women, but DCM Shriram helped me prove them wrong.

Swati Ahuja Deputy Manager - Technical Coordinator
Sugar Business, New Delhi
