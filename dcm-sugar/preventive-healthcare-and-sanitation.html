Preventive Healthcare & Sanitation

Khushali Sehat

Our flagship CSR program on preventive healthcare is running since 2010. It’s in alignment with various Government schemes like NURM, JSY, RKSK and ICDS. The Company also supports the cadre of outreach workers such as Frontline Health Workers (FHWs) ANMs, ASHA, Anganwadi to provide services related to health and nutrition.

The program works primarily towards Mother and Child health focusing on Women’s Reproductive Health with an objective to reduce IMR (Infant Mortality Rate) and MMR (Mothers Mortality Rate). Focus is also on improving the general health of the village community by increasing awareness and promoting support in Cataract operations.

Shriram Swachhagraha

The program is alignment with Govt. of India’s Swachh Bharat Mission, ODF+ and supporting Swachh Vidalaya Yojana and running in UP since 2014. The main objective of the program is to improve toilet usage in schools and strengthening system for routine maintenance of toilet blocks. The program’s overall objective is to implement a sustainable and holistic WASH program for the community and schools at large.
