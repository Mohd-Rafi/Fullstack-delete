Careers

A career at DCM Shriram is more than just a job. It’s a sense of deep purpose. It’s the comfort of working in an inclusive workplace. You will always be a part of supportive and empowering culture the will bring out the very best in you, everyday.,

Whether you are starting out, or have been on a career path, we have a road-map to take you further and faster, in a more fulfilling manner.

Steps of Our Selection Process
Step 01
Enrollment Stage

Vacancies are posted on DCM Shriram website in the careers section.

Step 02
Application Stage

Candidates fulfilling the mentioned criteria can apply through the link given

Step 03
Shortlist Stage

Based on the eligibility criteria our recruiters will shortlist the candidates for further selection process

Step 04
Selection Stage

Shortlisted candidates will undergo set of competency-based virtual & onsite interviews & behavioral assessments

Step 05
Onboarding Stage

Selected candidates will be sent offer letter & on joining they will undergo a comprehensive induction program

Step 06
Candidate Experience Survey Stage

We continuously strive to improve our process and hence will share questionnaire/ surveys at each stage to receive feedback

DCM Shriram welcomes applications from professional graduates in all disciplines who wish to seek challenging responsibilities and fulfilling career opportunities.

Please send us your profile using the Submit Resume link.
