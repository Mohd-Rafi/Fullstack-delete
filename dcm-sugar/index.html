Our Businesses

Sugar Power Biofuels

Setting benchmarks.
Catalysing change.

Situated in the heart of India’s sugarcane farming districts, partnering with over 2,25,000 growers, we produce about 650 million kilograms of sugar annually. Across 4 state-of-the-art, fully integrated sugar manufacturing units in Hardoi & Lakshimpur Kheri districts of Uttar Pradesh. Making us one of India’s leading sugar producers.

Maximising Green Power
through efficient technology

In a world where every resource often comes at a cost to the environment, we are creating captive energy sources and contributing to a more circular economy.

Making fuel greener.
Making a greener world.

At DCM Shriram , we use sugar by-products to manufacture Ethanol. This type of alcohol is a green fuel that can be blended with petroleum to lessen its impact on the environment. Helping to not only make a greener world but to also increase energy security for the nation by reducing dependence on crude oil imports.
