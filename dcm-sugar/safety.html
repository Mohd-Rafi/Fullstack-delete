At DCM Shriram we recognise the responsibility we have towards all who are involved with company operations or are touched by us in any way. We believe our employees should reach back home as safe as they left for work.

Accordingly, we have put in place a vision, objectives, strategies, systems, and training on health and safety. These are embedded across our operations, business processes, functions, and are a part of everything we do every day. We provide continuous support with safety tools and guidance. Empowering employees and associates with the skills and motivation to perform safely by following correct practices and safety systems.
