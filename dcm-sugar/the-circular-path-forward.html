The Circular Path Forward

Circular Economy Designed To Benefit All Stakeholders

At DCM Shriram we recognise that the sustainable way is the only way forward. Creating a culture of turning waste into value, our systems and manufacturing chain takes a 360 approach that ensures every part contributes to better whole. Integrating, optimising, investing in technology to create a path that designs out waste and maximises efficiency.

Every part of the sugar manufacturing cycle is put to use. And re-use. What was waste earlier is no longer waste but the seed for regeneration. The process ends up yielding power, green fuel as Ethanol, spirits, press mud and of course, sugar. Ensuring we make the most of every stick of Sugarcane, one of the most versatile and productive sources of renewable energy on Earth.

We have made the Circular Economy a part of our core philosophy. We are deeply committed to business grown with sustainability. Our ground-breaking initiatives have earned us respect and recognition as an environmentally conscious, socially responsible organisation that is being able to create real change on the ground and the grass-root level.
